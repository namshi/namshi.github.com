Design, Architecture and Code on the edge @Confoo

Written by Alessandro Cinelli (cirpo)

At Namshi we run a product that needs to evolve quickly and be ready to change: new features and technologies are integrated every week to be able to keep up to date with the market and the technology around us. That’s how we started using nodejs, Angular, Docker, SPDY and much more…

Do you want to hear more?

Alex submitted a bunch of talks to the 2015 edition of the Confoo conference, so just vote for his talks.
His talks cover a wide variety of topics from Symfony, Angular, HTTP to Dockerinzing your application.

How cool is that?
If you are looking for sharp and engaging sessions, these are definitely bound to make you sit up and notice.
